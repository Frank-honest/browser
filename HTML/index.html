Dr. Benjamin Eduafo-Arthur's Official Website

Dr. Benjamin Eduafo-Arthur is a dedicated and passionate Senior Lecturer in the Department of Mathematics at the University of Cape Coast (UCC). With over 20 years of experience in teaching and research, he has impacted countless students with his deep understanding of mathematical concepts and his unique teaching style.

He specializes in areas such as Calculus, Linear Algebra, and Pure Mathematics, and is committed to helping students not only pass but truly understand and appreciate the beauty of Mathematics. Through this website, students can access course materials, get updates on lectures, and connect for academic guidance.

Thank you for visiting, and feel free to explore the site to learn more about Dr. Benjamin Eduafo-Arthur's academic journey, research interests, and the courses he offers.
